Parking/Uber

  • Parking
    • The closest campus entrance to the Atmospheric Sciences Building is the WEST gate at 15th Ave NE and NE 40th St/Stevens Way.
    • Daytime on-campus parking is $15/day, with refunds for those that park less than 4 hours.
    • Nighttime (after 5pm) on-campus parking is $5/day.
    • UW Parking Info
  • Uber - If not renting a car and preferring not to walk, we highly recommend downloading the Uber app onto your phone and using it. Uber is very popular in the University District and, upon placing your request, your driver should pick you up in 5 minutes or less. The cost to and from the Watertown will be $4-6.
